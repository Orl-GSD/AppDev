About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed by miHoYo, published by miHoYo in mainland China and worldwide by Cognosphere. In the game, set forth on a journey across a fantasy world called Teyvat. In this vast world, you can explore different nations ruled by different archons, treasures, creatures, and companions to accompany you in your long journey. Moreover, the more you venture in this world, the more you unravel the mysteries of Teyvat, and yourself.

The game features an anime-style open-world environment and an action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching. Collect primogems, an in-game currency, to collect various characters and weapons to build your own dream team. Spend resin to collect materials from enemies and bosses, and artifacts to strengthen your characters.

Teyvat consists of 7 nations with its ruling Archon, Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru, Fontaine, Natlan, and Snezhnaya. The Archons granted mortals elemental abilities, particularly Pyro, Hydro, Anemo, Geo, Electro, and Cryo.

Mondstadt

Mondstadt is one the seven regions of Teyvat and is the first region where the Traveller starts his journey to find his long-lost sibling. It is the nation of wind and freedom, under the patronage of the Anemo Archon, Lord Barbatos.

Devoted to the ideals of freedom, Barbatos refused to institue direct rule over the nation, and only intervenes to protect the city's freedom in times of crisis. Mondstadt has no ruler, with the prominent Knights of the Favonius being only a civilian peacekeeping institution. The people of Mondstadt are instead guided by their belief in Lord Barbatos and the wings of freedom that he represents.

Liyue

Liyue is the nation of stone and contracts, under the patronage of the Geo Archon, Rex Lapis. A harbor-city located in eastern Teyvat, Liyue has been ruled by Rex Lapis for several millenia, who grew the nation into a bustling trade hub, and the most prosperous of the seven nations.

Though formally ruled by Rex Lapis, the archon only makes one annual public appearance at the Rite of Descension to dictate his policies. Daily governance is instead conducted by the Qixing, a committee of seven business leaders sworn to uphold the Rex's laws. His vision for Liyue is one of order - the creation of a nation where gods, immortals, and men can organize themselves through contract.

Inazuma

Inazuma is the nation of Lightning and Eternity, under the patronage of the Electro Archon, the Raiden Shogun. Situated on a secluded archipelago to the far east of Teyvat, Inazuma is at once beautiful and tarnished, its elegant culture and geograpy diluted by a past of warfare and disaster.

Ruled directly by Her Eternal Excellency, the Inazuma Shogunate loyally enforces her Sakoku and Vision Hunt Decrees, aiming to create an unchanging Inazuma through draconian laws. Though the outlying, autonomous Watatsumi Island rebelled against these harsh measures, the Shogun remains steadfast in her beliefs, her vision of true eternity unchanging and absolute.

Sumeru

Sumeru is the nation of nature and wisdom, nominally under the patronage of the Dendro Archon, Lesser Lord Kusalani. Located in west-central Teyvat, encopassing the lush Dharma Forest and the barren Great Red Sand, Sumeru owes its existence to Greater Lord Rukkhadevata, the previous Dendro Archon who founded Sumeru City and saved the jungle from desertification in ancient times.

Above the streets of Sumeru City stands the Akademiya, Teyvat's center of knowledge and Sumeru's center of authority. Kusanali has remained publicly absent after Rukkhadevata's passing, and the Sages of the Akademiya have continuously ruled Sumeru in her place since. With the gifts of Gods in their hands, the Sages pursued the riches of Infinite Wisdom, unguided and unopposed.

Fontaine

Fontaine is the nation of water and justice, under the patronage of the Hydro Archon, Focalors. However, she is not one of the original Seven Archons, only taking her place after the original Hydro Archon passed away. Nonetheless, she is incredibly popular - rather than governing her people, however, she seems mostly interested in Fontaine's famous opera scene.

Fontaine's culture is known for its opera and other types of performance arts and technological advancements, such as the aquabus transportation system. Unfortunately, not all is well in Fontaine as an ancient prophecy warns that the sea will swallow the sinful people of Fontaine, only allowing the Hydro Archon to survive. Although it was regarded as a mere legend, the waters of Fontaine have started to rise, suggesting that the prophecy may be true.

Natlan

Natlan is the nation of war and home to natural hot springs created from activity, and is under the patronage of the Pyro Archon in Teyvat. Moreover, it is known to be a land of the dragons, much like the Vishaps, but these creatures have evolved and some have been found a way to integrate human society to some degree. According to Neuvillette, war is constant in Natlan that ravages the land like an undying flame.

As of now, Natlan is not yet accessible and will be released in the future.

Snezhnaya

Snezhnaya is a region of perpetual winter storms and the nation that worships the Cryo Archon, Tsaritsa. the nation has a military force, the Fatui, under the control of the Tsaritsa and is particularly notorious for their diehard devotion and their unscrupulous methods in enacting her will.

As of now, Snezhnaya is not yet accessible and will be released in the future.
